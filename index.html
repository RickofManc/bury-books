Bury Books is an independent bookshop located in the market town of Bury in Greater Manchester, where it occupies a large and attractive traditional building right in the middle of the town's busy High Street. The shop was set up by Rick & Emma Atherton, in collaboration with local authors.

Bury Books has an extensive stock across all subject areas, including fiction, nature, technology and children's, serving the local community and visitors. Our Outdoor lounge hosts the largest selection of outdoor books in England.
